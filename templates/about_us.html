About Us

Our Mission

At Fencing Hub, our mission is to promote the sport of fencing by providing up-to-date rankings, event information, and engaging stories. We aim to create a community where fencers can connect, share their experiences, and stay informed about the latest developments in the sport.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Fencing Hub was created by a group of passionate fencers who saw the need for a centralized platform to bring together the global fencing community. Since then, we have grown to become a trusted source for fencing news, events, and rankings.

Join Our Community

We invite you to join our community and become a part of the Fencing Hub family. Whether you are a seasoned fencer or new to the sport, you will find valuable resources, support, and connections to help you on your fencing journey.
